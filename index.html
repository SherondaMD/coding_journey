How it happened

One day she was looking through the pages of LinkedIn, and she came across a SheCodes coding workshops for Women, post. She wondered if this was the ticket to her learning how to code. She made a mental note to check out the website and only after coming across three additional posts...she took it as a sign to check it out immediately. She joined one of the 60-minute introductory classes. ü§ì

Wow! It seemed to make so much sense to her, so why not fully give it a try. Either your code worked...or it didn't, and if it didn't...figure out how to make it work! She then signed up for the most basic class that she could take.

How it's going

5 long months passed, but she finished her courses and became certified as a Front-end Developer! She's never going to stop learning, and the prospect of having to learn more and more to keep up to date is exciting to her. Now she is looking to start a new career in tech as a developer while still looking for new things to learn about. The top three items on her list are - Python, React Native, and to begin her Full Stack studies.
